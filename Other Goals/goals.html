Change What You Measure

To start your program off right, we recommend setting small goals to build momentum. Don't feel like you have to dramatically change all of your habits overnight, since slow and steady is the best way to do it! That's why the Fast Break stage (picking a few small goals to focus on for the first two weeks of your program) is so important. To get started, choose 1 Fast Break goals from the list below (one from each area). These are things you should try to do every day for the next two weeks.
